Rewind Survivor

Having an issue with Rewind Survivor? Here are some common solutions:

Game progress lost: Make sure iCloud is enabled on your device under Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud. Game progress syncs automatically when connected.

Performance issues: Try closing other apps running in the background and restarting the game. The game is optimized for iPhone and iPad.

App crashes on launch: Ensure your device is running iOS 15.6 or later. If the issue persists, try deleting and reinstalling the app.
