Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 28, 2026

First Coast Games ("we", "us", or "our") develops and publishes mobile games. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our apps.

Information We Collect

Our games do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

Data Stored on Your Device

Game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard system storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device except through Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store if you have iCloud enabled, which syncs your game progress across your own devices. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure and we have no access to it.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising frameworks. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or any form of telemetry.

Third-Party Services

Our games do not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

Our games do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Our games contain no user-generated content, social features, or external links.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
